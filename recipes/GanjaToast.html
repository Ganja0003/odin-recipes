Ganja Toast

This is one of my favorite dishes and is named after my nickname Ganja

it is very rich in flavour and i havent seen people who doesnt like the pleasant taste amd almost everyone will become addicted to it ones they try it

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 5 toast bread
  • 50 ml milk
  • 20g butter
  • 50g sugar

Steps:

  1. start of by prepare your frying pan by letting the butter melt in the mean time with medium heat
  2. break 3 eggs and then stir them with 50 ml milk and 50g sugar
  3. then get your 5 breads and then turn them around on the egg mass
  4. then fry all of them at the same if they can fit obviously mine can
  5. then serve with milk an you are good to go
